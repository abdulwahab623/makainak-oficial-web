Privacy Policy

How we securely collect, use, and protect your personal information at Makainak.

Last Updated: May 18, 2026

  • 2. Info We Collect

1. Introduction

Welcome to Makainak ("we," "our," or "us"). As Pakistan's premier on-demand home maintenance platform connecting you with verified professionals in Faisalabad, Lahore, Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Multan, and beyond, your trust is our ultimate asset.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, store, use, and protect your personal information when you access our website, book services (such as plumbing, electrical, solar installation, and AC repair), and use our customer dashboards. By utilizing our secure connection, you consent to the practices described in this document.

Secure 256-bit Connection

We employ strict industry-standard SSL encryption protocols to protect all data transferred during signup, sign-in, booking, and checkout processes.

2. Information We Collect

To provide robust, efficient, and background-checked home services, we collect several types of details. This includes information you provide directly and details collected automatically:

A. Direct User Input
  • Identity & Account Details: Your full name, secure password, and profile preferences collected when creating an account.
  • Contact Information: Active email address and phone number for booking updates, security OTP verifications, and general correspondence.
  • Location & Dispatch Data: Home or business address, city, and GPS coordinates to match and dispatch the closest verified technician to your site.
  • Booking & Invoice Files: Issue descriptions, photos/media uploaded to illustrate required repairs, and billing invoices (such as bank transfer slips uploaded for verification during checkout).
B. Technical Usage Data
  • Device Specifications: IP addresses, browser specifications, operating system versions, and unique device IDs.
  • Session Operations: Service category selections, click behaviors, navigation timings, cart interactions, and search queries (e.g. searching "What service do you want!").

3. How We Use Information

Makainak uses your information strictly to support, automate, and secure your end-to-end service journey:

  • Service Matching & Booking: Connecting your service requests (domestic or corporate) with licensed plumbers, electricians, or civil engineers.
  • Real-Time Tracking: Offering real-time location mapping of dispatched service professionals directly to your customer dashboard.
  • Payment & Billing Validation: Handling Cash-on-Service options and securely verifying Bank Transfer detail matches against uploaded invoices.
  • Enterprise Security: Facilitating secure sign-in controls, SSO integrations, device trust tracking, and Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) enforcement.
  • Performance Optimization: Analyzing site usability and feature logs to optimize our responsive layout across devices.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

Your privacy is non-negotiable. Makainak strictly limits data access and will only disclose relevant details under the following contexts:

  • With Dispatched Service Providers: Only sharing critical coordination information (name, phone, address, AC/electrical issue details) with the background-checked professional assigned to execute your booking.
  • Secure Payment Gateways: Transmitting necessary details securely to designated banks or payment gateways when validating online transfers.
  • Legal & Safety Mandates: Disclosing information only if strictly required under state laws of Pakistan or to enforce platform safety guarantees.
Strict "No-Sale" Policy

We never rent, lease, trade, sell, or monetize your personal identity data, location logs, or invoice files with third-party advertising brokers or aggregators.

5. Data Security

Makainak operates enterprise-grade protection frameworks designed to safeguard all collected user assets:

  • Encrypted Transport: Full site-wide SSL coverage ensuring that login details, billing records, and personal profiles are secure from external capture.
  • MFA Infrastructure: Support for user-enforced Multi-Factor Authentication upon secure login sessions.
  • Access Auditing: Restricting customer address details and phone numbers behind strict access matrices, accessible only by verified support specialists and active booking personnel.

6. Your Rights & Controls

We grant you complete authority over how your personal details are updated, customized, and utilized:

  • Profile Customization: Directly update your billing addresses, password credentials, and personal profiles within your Customer Dashboard.
  • Device Management: Revoke session access and request device status validation to ensure no unauthorized sign-in attempts occur.
  • Account Deactivation: Request complete cleanup and deletion of your service logs, location coordinates, and registered email references by contacting our security helpdesk.

7. Contact Support

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, your secure connection parameters, or wish to exercise your data protection rights, please reach out to our team:

  • Direct Web Form: Submit requests directly through our Help & Contact Center.
  • Operational Support: Operational security managers are stationed at Faisalabad, Lahore, and Islamabad offices.
  • Email Reference: security@makainak.com
