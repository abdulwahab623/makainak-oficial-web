10 Tips to Keep Your AC Running Efficiently This Summer

By Makainak Expert May 2, 2026 • 5 min read

As the temperatures soar, your Air Conditioning system becomes your best friend. However, without proper care, it can also become a source of high electricity bills and unexpected repairs. Here are 10 expert tips to keep your AC running at its peak performance throughout the summer season.

1. Clean or Replace Air Filters Regularly

A clogged filter restricts airflow and significantly reduces efficiency. During heavy usage months, check your filters every 30 days and clean or replace them as needed.

2. Keep the Outdoor Unit Clear

Your AC's outdoor condenser unit needs room to breathe. Ensure there's at least 2 feet of clearance around it, removing any leaves, debris, or overgrown plants.

3. Schedule Professional Maintenance

An annual check-up by a certified professional can catch small issues before they become major disasters. Makainak offers comprehensive AC general services to ensure your system is summer-ready.

"Prevention is always better—and cheaper—than repair. A well-maintained AC can last up to 15 years while saving you up to 20% on energy costs."
